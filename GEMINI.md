This is a full-stack application for a trading journal and analytics platform called Tradstry.

**Project Structure:**

*   **Frontend:** Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS. Located in `app/`, `components/`, `hooks/`, `lib/`.
*   **Backend:** Python, FastAPI. Located in `backend/`.
*   **Database:** PostgreSQL. SQL scripts are in `Database /`.
